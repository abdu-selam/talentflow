FAQs.

Find answers to the most common questions about TalentFlow. Whether you’re a job seeker or an employer, we make it easy to understand how our platform works.

  • How do I create a profile?

    Sign up and fill in your skills, experience, and portfolio to complete your profile.

  • Can I apply to multiple jobs?

    Yes! TalentFlow lets you apply to as many jobs as you qualify for.

  • Is TalentFlow free for job seekers?

    Yes, creating a profile and applying to jobs is completely free.

  • How do I track my applications?

    Go to your dashboard to see the status of all applications in one place.

  • Can I update my profile later?

    Absolutely! You can edit your skills, experience, and portfolio anytime.

  • How do I post a job?

    Sign up as an employer, create a company profile, and fill out the job posting form.

  • Can I edit a job after posting?

    Yes, you can update job details, requirements, or deadlines anytime from your dashboard.

  • How do I find suitable candidates?

    Use TalentFlow’s search and filtering tools to browse candidate profiles efficiently.

  • Is there a limit on job postings?

    No, you can post as many jobs as needed to find the right talent.

  • Can I communicate with candidates directly?

    Yes, you can message applicants through TalentFlow to schedule interviews or ask questions.
